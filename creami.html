Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 cup fat-free milk
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 tbsp sugar-free instant pudding mix
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp granulated sweetener

Method

  1. Whisk all ingredients in the pint until fully dissolved and smooth.
  2. Freeze level on a flat shelf for 24 hours.
  3. Spin on the Lite Ice Cream setting.
  4. If powdery, add a splash of milk and re-spin once.
Tip: The pudding mix is the secret to a creamy, scoopable texture without extra fat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp vanilla bean paste
  • 3 tbsp cream cheese, softened

Method

  1. Warm milk and sugar until sugar dissolves, then cool.
  2. Whisk in cream, vanilla paste, and cream cheese until smooth.
  3. Freeze in the pint for 24 hours.
  4. Spin on the Ice Cream setting.
Tip: A spoon of cream cheese smooths the texture and tames iciness.

  • 2 tbsp lime juice
